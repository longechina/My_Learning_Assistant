1. Which of the following best describes the term **subjection** in a legal context?  
   A) A voluntary agreement between parties  
   B) The act of imposing a legal duty or control over someone  
   C) A type of civil court proceeding  
   D) A religious rite performed by a tribunal  

2. The new law will place all lower courts under the ______ of the Supreme Tribunal.  
   ____  

3. Translate the following sentence into Chinese:  
   "The tribunal's decision was final and could not be appealed."

4. Find and correct the mistake in the sentence below:  
   "The tribunal was subjection to the new regulations imposed by the parliament."

5. Arrange the following words to form a meaningful sentence:  
   tribunal – subjection – appeal – justice  

---
